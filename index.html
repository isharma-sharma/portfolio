About Me

My name is Pooja and I am a transplant from India that moved to Boston on a whim in 2016,I am always finding the silver lining, striving to learn new things.

My zest for challenge and self-improvement led me to the Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly, where I built three full-stack web applications and one front-end SPA using modern technologies and frameworks. I'm most adept using HTML5, CSS3, Handlebars, Javascript, SQL, and Ruby on Rails. At the end of the day, I love to laugh, and work hard alongside like-minded people.

Please check out my projects and feel free to reach out if you have any questions, want to work together or discuss an available opportunity, or just to say "hi"!
